The Band

Dirt, Alice in Chains tribute, are based in the UK, and bring you the you the sound and feel of Seattle’s finest grunge metal band, with energetic live performances. They have played in all parts of the UK over rthe last 5 years and are available for live shows across the UK and Ireland. Our line up includes Mark Southcombe Lead Singer and Rythym guitarist, Sam Bateman Lead guitarist, backing vocals, Andrew Gibbons on Bass Guitar and Rhodri William-Jones,Drummer, backing vocals.
